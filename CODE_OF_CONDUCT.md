# Code of Conduct

## In short

Be kind. Assume good faith. Critique ideas, not people. Don't drag personal disputes into Issues or PRs. If a conversation here would feel inappropriate at a professional conference, take it to private channels or end it.

## Scope

This applies to all interactions in this repository's Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and any project-related space.

## Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behavior may be filed to the repository author via the email or contact info on the GitHub profile. The author will respond and act in good faith. There is no formal committee — this is a one-person research project — so enforcement is light-touch by necessity. Egregious behavior results in being blocked from the repo.
